## Image Quality Requirements

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Format:** JPG (JPEG quality 90%+)
- **File Size:** 1-5 MB per image
- **Lighting:** Natural light or well-lit (no flash if possible)
- **Background:** Plain/neutral preferred
- **Focus:** Sharp, clear (not blurry)

### Composition Guidelines
- [ ] Product centered in frame
- [ ] Fill 70-80% of frame (not too small)
- [ ] Multiple angles per item (front, side, detail)
- [ ] No hands/people in frame
- [ ] No strong shadows
- [ ] Item fully visible (not cut off)

## Etiquette & Professionalism

### Do's
✅ Arrive during non-peak hours (10-11 AM weekdays)
✅ Dress professionally (university attire)
✅ Ask permission before photographing
✅ Thank staff and offer to share final research
✅ Be quick and efficient (respect their time)
✅ Offer to blur any sensitive info if needed

### Don'ts
❌ Don't block customers
❌ Don't touch items without permission
❌ Don't take photos of customers/staff
❌ Don't share images online before permission
❌ Don't be pushy if they say no to something
